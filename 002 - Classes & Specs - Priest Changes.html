Voice of Faith

A Redesign of the Priest Class

Torn between the divine Light and the ravenous Void, the Priest walks a path of perilous balance. We amplify the distinct doctrines of each specialization, ensuring that whether they preserve, protect, or punish, their convictions are made manifest with awesome power.

Preserve, Protect, Punish

The Priest is the quintessential wielder of spiritual power, a master of healing and shadow. Our redesign sharpens the identity of each covenant: Discipline becomes the ultimate proactive protector, Holy the unmatched reactive healer, and Shadow a far more self-sufficient and devastating damage dealer.
