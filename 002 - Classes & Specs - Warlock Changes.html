The Dark Harvest

A Redesign of the Warlock Class

Power has its price. We delve into the forbidden arts to refine the Warlock, ensuring that their command over afflictions, demons, and raw destruction is not only potent but also mechanically compelling and true to their dark pacts.

Corruption, Command, and Chaos

The Warlock is a master of dark power, bringing indispensable utility and devastating damage to any raid. Our philosophy enhances the distinct identity of each pact: Affliction's slow decay, Demonology's symbiotic mastery, and Destruction's overwhelming force are all polished to a razor's edge.
