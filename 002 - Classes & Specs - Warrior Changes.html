The Unbroken

A Redesign of the Warrior Class

By rage and steel, a warrior's path is forged. We delve into the heart of battle to redefine the master of combat, ensuring every shout, every charge, and every swing of the blade is an earth-shattering testament to their power.

Tactics, Rage, and Steel

The Warrior is the heart of any assault and the cornerstone of any defense. While masters of their craft in TBC, we aim to refine their specializations further, enhancing their rotational depth, group utility, and overall battlefield presence to make each spec feel as legendary as their wielder.

Modern Ability: Bladestorm:

A new capstone talent. Become an unstoppable storm of steel, hitting all nearby enemies and granting immunity to crowd control. The ultimate AoE burst cooldown.

Your melee hits have a chance to allow the use of Execute on the target, regardless of their health. A thrilling proc that adds execute-phase pressure throughout the fight.

The frenzied berserker, a whirlwind of steel and rage. We temper their late-game scaling while improving their early-game consistency and threat management.

Core Fixes üîß

Improved Rage Generation:

New talents are introduced to improve early-game rage generation from critical strikes, making the leveling and gearing process smoother.

Threat Mitigation:

A new passive talent reduces the threat generated by your core abilities, Bloodthirst and Whirlwind, allowing you to unleash your full fury with less risk of pulling aggro.

Modern Ability: Raging Blow:

A powerful instant strike that becomes usable only when you are Enraged. It adds a hard-hitting, reactive button to your rotation during your most furious moments.

Iconic Overhaul: Titan's Grip:

A conceptual but game-changing 41-point talent. Allows the warrior to wield two two-handed weapons, fulfilling the ultimate fantasy of the unstoppable juggernaut.

Target: Improve AoE Threat & Group Utility

The unyielding bulwark, commander of the battlefield. We bolster their AoE threat generation and grant them powerful new tools to control their enemies and protect their allies.

Core Fixes & Buffs üîß

Superior AoE Threat:

Thunder Clap's damage and threat are significantly increased. The cooldown on Shield Slam is reduced, and Devastate applies its debuff faster, improving threat on all fronts.

Enhanced Cooldowns:

Key survival cooldowns like Shield Wall and Last Stand have their timers reduced. Utility stuns like Concussion Blow are also more readily available.

Power Infusions ‚ú®

Modern Ability: Shockwave:

A new talent that sends a wave of force in a cone, stunning all enemies hit. This provides incredible control and snap threat on multi-mob pulls.

Modern Ability: Mass Spell Reflection:

A game-changing talent that reflects the next spell cast on you AND all nearby party members. Unparalleled utility against caster bosses and trash.

Conceptual Mitigation: Shield Barrier:

An optional talent to spend Rage on a damage absorption shield, providing an active mitigation tool for magic damage or predictable damage spikes.
