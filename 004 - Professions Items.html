Profession Overhaul: Utility, Identity, & Engagement

This proposal redesigns TBC professions to be more than just stat sticks or mandatory raid consumables. The goal is to enhance parity, encourage diversity, and add engaging gameplay loops through unique utilities, epic crafting quests, and system refinements, ensuring every artisan's craft is valuable.

Masterwork Crafting Quests üìú

To provide a meaningful progression path outside of raiding, each crafting profession receives an epic, lore-rich questline. These quests involve gathering rare materials, completing objectives in TBC dungeons, and collaborating with other professions. The journey culminates in crafting a unique, Bind-on-Pickup item competitive with T5/early T6 gear, representing the pinnacle of your chosen craft.

On-Use Utility Items

Each profession gains unique, situational on-use items. These provide tangible party/raid utility, encouraging diverse profession choices and rewarding player investment. They do not share cooldowns with potions or trinkets, but have their own cooldowns to prevent spamming.
