The Untamed Wild

A Redesign of the Hunter Class

From the shadowed forests of Azeroth to the shattered plains of Outland, the Hunt is eternal. We now refine the very essence of the master predator, honing their skills to ensure their legend is sung in every tavern and feared on every battlefield.

One Shot, One Kill, One Purpose

The Hunter is a master of ranged combat and feral companionship. The symbiotic bond between hunter and beast, forged in the heart of the wild. We strengthen this connection, ensuring the pet is a resilient and devastating extension of the master's will.

Core Fixes üîß

Pet Survivability Overhaul:

Pets gain a massive baseline boost to Stamina and Armor scaling and now take 50% reduced damage from non-player AoE, making them far more resilient in raids.

Smarter, Faster Companions:

Pet AI pathing and responsiveness are significantly improved, reducing delays and making them re-engage targets much more quickly after crowd control or movement.

Power Infusions ‚ú®

Modern Ability: Kill Command Rework:

Replaces the old passive effect with an active, instant command for your pet to deal high damage, making the ability more interactive and impactful.

New Talent: Barbed Shot:

A new shot that applies a bleed and enrages your pet, increasing its attack speed. This adds a compelling DoT management layer to the BM rotation.

New Talent: Animal Companion:

An optional talent allowing you to summon a second pet from your stable to fight alongside your main one, adding immense visual flair and passive cleave damage.

Marksmanship üéØ

Target DPS Buff: +4-8%

The patient sniper who finds and exploits weaknesses from afar. We sharpen their focus, reduce cast times, and reward precision with devastating burst potential.

Core Fixes üîß

Aimed Shot Refined:

The base cast time of Aimed Shot is reduced from 3.0s to 2.5s, making it a more fluid and less punishing part of the rotation.

Deadzone Eliminated:

The frustrating 5-8 yard melee deadzone is removed. Auto-shot can now be used within melee range, providing a massive quality-of-life improvement.

Scaling Trueshot Aura:

Trueshot Aura now scales dynamically, adding a portion of the Hunter's own Attack Power to the raid buff, making their gear progression more valuable to the group.

Power Infusions ‚ú®

New Capstone: Master Sniper:

A new 41-point talent giving your Steady and Aimed Shot critical strikes a chance to reset the cooldown of Aimed Shot and make it instant cast, creating thrilling burst cycles.

Modern Ability: Chimera Shot (from WotLK):

(As an alternate talent path) An instant shot that refreshes your Serpent Sting and deals weapon damage, providing a powerful, synergistic signature ability.

Survival üå≤

Target: No DPS Adjustment (Focus on Utility/Mechanics)

The cunning trapper and master of tactical explosives. We transform Survival from a niche support spec into a dynamic and explosive damage dealer with unique utility.

Core Fixes üîß

Trap Launcher Baseline:

Hunters can now throw traps from 40 yards away via a new baseline ability, finally allowing them to use their core utility safely and effectively in combat.

Improved Traps:

Traps can now be placed while in combat. Their trigger radius and responsiveness are improved, making them far more reliable tools of control.

Power Infusions ‚ú®

Modern Ability: Explosive Shot:

This iconic WotLK ability becomes the core rotational shot for Survival, firing an explosive charge that deals high fire damage over time and scales with Attack Power.

Modern Talent: Lock and Load:

Triggering your traps has a chance to make your next two Explosive Shots free and have no cooldown, creating an exciting, proc-based gameplay loop that rewards clever trap usage.

Modern Talent: Black Arrow:

A new talent that fires a shadowy DoT. While active, your periodic trap damage can reset the cooldown of Explosive Shot, adding another layer of synergy to your kit.
