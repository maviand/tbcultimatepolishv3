The Silver Hand

A Redesign of the Paladin Class

The Light does not waver. It does not falter. We channel its divine power to reforge its chosen champions, ensuring that whether they stand as a bastion of protection, a beacon of hope, or an instrument of righteous fury, their purpose is clear and their might is absolute.

Faith, Fury, and Fortitude

The Paladin's fantasy in The Burning Crusade was fractured. Holy and Protection were cornerstones of raiding, while Retribution was a legend in its own right‚Äîfor all the wrong reasons. Modern Ability: Ardent Defender:

This talent is reworked into a "cheat death" mechanic. An attack that would kill you is negated, reducing you to 15% health instead. A true last stand.

New Capstone: Guardian of Ancient Kings:

A new 41-point talent. Summon a spirit guardian for 12 seconds that reduces damage taken by the Paladin and nearby allies. A powerful personal and group defensive cooldown.

The holy warrior, judging foes with righteous fury. We fix their mechanical flaws, address their mana issues, and unleash their potential to be a true credit to the team, not just a buff.

Core Fixes üîß

Reliable Seals & Judgements:

Seal of Command procs are normalized for consistency and scale better. Judgements now refresh Seals automatically, a massive quality-of-life improvement.

Mana Management Solution:

Key abilities have their mana costs reduced through talents, and Judgement of the Wise's mana return is increased, finally addressing the spec's chronic mana starvation.

Modern Ability: Divine Storm:

This iconic talent replaces Repentance (which becomes baseline), hitting nearby enemies and healing allies. This provides Retribution with desperately needed AoE damage and cleave healing.

Streamlined "Hand" System:

Short-term blessings (Freedom, Protection, etc.) are converted to a "Hand" system, simplifying buff management and allowing for more tactical application in combat.
